Frequently Asked Questions

What is product authentication, and why do I need it?

Product authentication is the process of verifying the authenticity of a product to ensure it is genuine and not counterfeit. You need it to protect yourself from purchasing fake or substandard products that may pose safety risks or lead to financial losses.

How does your website work?

Our website utilizes advanced web-scraping technology to gather information from various online sources about the product you're interested in. We then analyze and cross-reference this data to provide you with accurate details about the product's company, price, description, and other relevant information.

Is your website free to use?

Yes, our basic product authentication service is entirely free to use. We may offer premium features or subscription plans iin the future for additional benefits, but the core service shall remain free of charge.

Is web scraping legal?

Web scraping is a complex legal topic that varies depending on the website's terms of service and the jurisdiction you're in. As a user, you should be aware of the website's policies and use our service responsibly. We respect the terms of service of the websites we scrape data from and aim to comply with all relevant laws and regulations.

Can I report inaccurate information or suggest new products for authentication?

Yes, we welcome user feedback and appreciate any reports of inaccurate information. You can contact our customer support or use the provided feedback mechanism to suggest new products or report any discrepancies you encounter.
